# Introducing NFC Tool - A Powerful NFC Reader and Emulator App

I'm excited to share one of my most significant projects: **NFC Tool**, a comprehensive Android application designed for reading, analyzing, and emulating NFC cards and tags. Whether you're working with payment cards, identification cards, or exploring NFC technology, this app provides the professional tools you need.

## What is NFC Tool?

NFC Tool is a feature-rich Android application that specializes in:
- **Reading various NFC cards** including payment cards and CIC (Citizen Identity Cards)
- **Virtual NFC tag emulation** using Host Card Emulation (HCE)
- **Advanced NFC communication** with minimal system popup interference
- **Multiple NFC technology support** (NfcA, NfcB, IsoDep, MifareClassic, etc.)
- **Professional card analysis** with detailed technical data
